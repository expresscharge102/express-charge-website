ABOUT EXPRESS CHARGE

Built from real road experience.

Express Charge is a Romanian company focused on express road transport and freight forwarding across Europe.

Established in 2013 and active in international road transport since 2022, we combine practical transport experience with a long-term vision for reliable logistics partnerships.

Today, we coordinate flexible solutions for shipments from 1 to 10 pallets through our own operational capacity and a growing network of trusted transport collaborators.
