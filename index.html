ABOUT EXPRESS CHARGE

Connecting Europe with reliable express transport solutions.

Express Charge is a Romanian company focused on express road transport and freight forwarding across Europe.

Established in 2013 and active in international road transport since 2022, we combine practical road experience with a long-term vision for dependable logistics partnerships.
